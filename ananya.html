UK House Music

Have you ever heard of U.K. House Music? U.K. House Music is what is played in clubs by DJs. House music was created in and by the African American community. It has origins in gospel, soul, and funk. The house music came to the U.K. as Chicago House Music. Speaking of which, in Chicago, the Warehouse is one of the most popular places to listen to house music. Larry Levan was the first person to suggest creating the Warehouse.
